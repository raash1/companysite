I've been playing many different sports since I was a young child. Anything, from cheerleading to basketball. When I turned 10 years old, I decided to start playing golf.
Inspiration: I was inspired by my dad to play golf. He started playing golf a few years after moving to the United States and loved it. I used to go to the range and watch him practice. Later, my dad put me in the First Tee classes which helped me get better and establish my golf principles and life skills. I've been playing it for years since then. Freshman year, I tried out for the highschool girls golf team and made it to varsity. I hope to continue playing this sport all my life!
